Fluffy Pancakes (no buttermilk)

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour (sifted)
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3/4 cup + 2 tsp milk
  • 1 large egg, beaten until fluffy
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • vegetable oil for cooking

Method

  1. Combine dry and wet ingredients in 2 separate bowls. Stir to combine.
  2. Add wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir gently to mix, until just combined. Do not overwork
  3. Leave in fridge for 10-15mins to rise.
  4. Use a 1/2 cup to scoop batter into a pre-heated cast-iron skillet and cover with a lid to allow pancake to rise.
